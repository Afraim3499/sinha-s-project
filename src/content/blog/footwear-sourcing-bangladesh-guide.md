While Bangladesh is synonymous with garments, its footwear industry is a significant pillar of the export economy. In 2026, the country has solidified its position as a top-tier destination for leather shoes, sports footwear, and sustainable sandals, driven by a domestic leather supply and a growing technical workforce.

### The Footwear Clusters: Dhaka & Beyond
Footwear production is concentrated in specialized industrial zones around Dhaka and Gazipur. These aren't just converted garment factories; they are purpose-built facilities equipped with modern machinery specifically designed for the complexities of shoe lasting and sole injection.

### Why Bangladesh for Footwear?

-   **Backward Integration (Leather)**: Bangladesh produces high-quality leather. Having the tannery and the shoe factory in the same region significantly reduces material lead times.
-   **Trade Advantages**: Like garments, footwear from Bangladesh enjoys favorable duty-free access to many international markets, providing a strategic landed-cost advantage.
-   **Technical Diversification**: Beyond leather, factories are now excelling in **Synthetic Footwear** (PU/PVC) and **Athletic Shoes**, catering to the volume needs of global retailers.

### 3 Tips for Footwear Buyers
1.  **Verify the Lasts**: Ensure the factory has experience with your specific regional sizing standards (UK/US/EU).
2.  **Audit the Chemicals**: If sourcing leather, ensure the tannery meets international environmental standards (such as LWG certification).
3.  **Invest in Prototyping**: Footwear development is technically intensive. Invest more time in the initial mold and prototype stage to ensure production accuracy.
